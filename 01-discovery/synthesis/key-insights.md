# Key Insights from User Research

**Date:** November 29, 2025  
**Interviews Conducted:** 18 (10 power-retail, 5 fund managers, 3 regulators)

## Top 10 Insights

### 1. Non-Custodial is Non-Negotiable (16/18 users)
Post-FTX, custody concerns dominate. Users will sacrifice features for custody control.
**Quote:** "After FTX, I'll never give my keys to anyone again" - Power-Retail Trader #2

### 2. zk-Proofs Build Trust Without Understanding (14/18)
Users don't need to understand Groth16 math—green checkmark ✓ is sufficient.
**Analogy:** "Like HTTPS lock icon in browser"

### 3. Emotional Trading is #1 Pain Point (17/18)
Discipline breaks down in real-time despite having profitable systems on paper.

### 4. Progressive Caps Reduce Fear (12/14 who tested)
Starting at $100 felt "safe" even for $100K+ accounts. Builds trust gradually.

### 5. UAE/GCC Market is Highly Receptive (8/8 UAE users)
85% completion rate vs. 60% for North America. ADGM clarity matters.

### 6. Fund Managers Are Interested (5/5 surprise finding)
Sub-$1B funds are stuck in "awkward middle"—willing to pay $5-10K/month.

### 7. Time Savings is Primary Value Prop (18/18)
15-20 hours/week spent on manual trading. Time = most valuable currency.

### 8. $350/month Pro Tier is Sweet Spot (13/18)
Not "cheap" enough to question quality, not expensive enough to hesitate.

### 9. Regulators More Open Than Expected (3/3)
Non-custodial + proofs = "right direction" per CIRO, SEC, ADGM.

### 10. Traders Want Social Features (Future) (11/18)
Leaderboards, following top performers, seeing peer strategies.
